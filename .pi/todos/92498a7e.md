Make the game playable on phones and tablets.

- Detect touch device and split the screen into left/right touch zones
- Drag finger up/down in a zone to move that paddle (paddle.y follows touch.y, clamped)
- Tap anywhere to serve (replaces Space)
- Hide the keyboard help text on touch devices, show a touch hint instead
- Make sure `touch-action: none` is set on the canvas to prevent scroll/zoom

Files: `src/main.js` (pointer events), `index.html` (CSS `touch-action`, hint text).
